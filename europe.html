Europe

Florence For the artsy

The cradle of Reneissance, Florence! It's a city for all! You will leave this city inspired.
On one corner a woman strums the strings of a guitar, diagnolly across you find a man using the asphalt as his canvas, and a couple steps down you look up and witness Brunelleschi's dome and Giotto's Bell Tower, pieces of art that record moments of greatness. Whether you admire Michelangelo's sculpture of david or fall in awe with Boticelli's 'The Birth Of Venus,' it will dawn upon that all these master pieces are the result of a lot of small things done well everyday, and that you are capable of greatness too!

LondonFor architectural buffs

London, also known as Londinium by the Romans, is a majestic place.
A mixture of Modern, Medieval, and Reneissance architecture. A city full of history. Many of the best-designed buildings in Europe and the world can be found in London. As well as well-known architectural landmarks like St. Paul's Cathedral, Big Ben, and Westminster Abbey, the city is home to a plethora of impeccably designed buildings and structures.
